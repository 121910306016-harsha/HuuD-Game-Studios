What We Do

Hello there! Our main goal here at HuuD Game Studios is to develop games which will have you hooked and are unique from the extensive range of games available. Our intention is to develop games which interest, inspire and challenge players in all the ways possible and provide novelty in concepts. We plan to dive into all the genres of games that exist and more and we want to cater to all kinds of players. We want to explore different game mechanisms and everything there is to know about them. The main idea here is to develop games which entertain, excite and most importantly create joyful and memorable gaming experiences which make a difference.

Spunk'em

Spunk'em is a 3d arcade style space shooter game, defeat enemy spaceships and make your score for every spaceship destroyed and make high score, with the score you have made, that score gets converted to galaxy coins with which you can buy other spaceships, unlock all 13 spaceships and create mass destruction.
